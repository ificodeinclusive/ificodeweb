OUR STORY

Introduction to iFiCode Inclusive

Built in Kathmandu with a clear purpose: make modern digital work feel sharper, faster, and more useful for growing teams.

Feb 2025 Concept phase

The idea took shape around practical, scalable digital solutions.

Jun 2025 Formal launch

iFiCode Inclusive began operating as an innovation-focused digital partner.

iFiCode Inclusive was formally established in June 2025, following its initial concept phase in February 2025. From the outset, the vision has been clear: to work as a forward-thinking innovation partner, not just another IT service provider.

Our work is shaped around three focused pillars: Digital Solutions & Experiences, Applied Research & Insights, and Visionary Innovation & Experiments. Together, they help us design websites, platforms, insights, and digital systems that are precise, scalable, and useful.

Grounded in professionalism and driven by innovation, iFiCode Inclusive helps businesses strengthen their digital presence, adopt emerging technologies, and move toward the future with confidence.
